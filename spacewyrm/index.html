Space Wyrm's origins trace back to the 8-bit game Snake — now upgraded to a dragon flying through space.

Along with the upgraded playing field and character is an upgraded steering system. Instead of pressing buttons, or even using a joystick, you steer your dragon simply by tilting your device. The system is designed after airplane steering. Check out the Help page to read more, and watch a video that demonstrates the game's steering.
